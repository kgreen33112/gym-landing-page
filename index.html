About Us

Forge Fitness was built on the idea that everyone deserves a place to feel supported, challenged, and encouraged. We’re more than just a gym — we’re a community of people working together to improve our health, confidence, and mindset one workout at a time. No matter your experience level, our coaches meet you where you are and help you grow at your own pace. We celebrate progress over perfection and believe fitness should be sustainable, empowering, and fun.
